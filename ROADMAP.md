### VC Mode Enhancements

#### AI & Competition
- ‚úÖ **More AI Personalities** - Added 4 new AI players with distinct strategies:

  - **Lightspeed Venture Partners** (consumer_focused) - Consumer products focus

- **Historical Head-to-Head Records** - Track wins/losses vs specific AI opponents

#### Analytics & Tools
- ‚úÖ **Portfolio Dashboard** - Enhanced with carry interest projections, hurdle tracking, and detailed financial metrics
- ‚úÖ **ROI Predictor** - Projected ROI for each investment - Analyzes growth trends, provides best/worst case scenarios with confidence levels
- **Risk Calculator** - Probability of different outcomes
- **Market Trends Visualization** - Visualize sector trends over time
- **Exit Simulator** - Predict when companies might exit

### Founder Mode Enhancements

#### Strategic Features
- ‚úÖ **Competitor AI** - AI competitors based on Silicon Valley TV show startups:
  - **20 Silicon Valley Companies**: Hooli, Nucleus, End Frame, Aviato, Bream-Hall, Raviga, and more
  - **Strategic AI Behaviors**: Steal customers, launch competing products, aggressive pricing, poach talent
  - **Hooli-Specific Actions**: Massive marketing campaigns, patent lawsuits, executive poaching, conference announcements
  - **Acquisition Logic**: Competitors can acquire your startup with company-specific behaviors (Hooli lowballs, Nucleus competes aggressively)
  - **Dynamic Threat Levels**: Competitors grow stronger if ignored, can be defeated if competed against
- **Product Features Roadmap** - Plan and execute product feature development
- **Customer Testimonials** - Case studies and testimonials system
- **Competitive Intelligence** - Gather intel on competitors
- **Investor Relations Management** - Manage investor communications
- **PR & Media Coverage** - Media events and press coverage mechanics

#### Board & Governance Features
- ‚úÖ **Board Meeting Interface** - Visual board table view with animations when holding board meetings - Implemented with ASCII art board table, member visualization, integrated into vote handling
- ‚úÖ **View Current Board Members** - See all board members (investors, advisors, independent directors) - Implemented in portfolio dashboard and separate view function
- ‚úÖ **Fire Board Members** - As majority owner, remove board members (with consequences) - Implemented with 51%+ ownership requirement, serious consequences
- ‚úÖ **Set Advisor as Chairman** - Appoint an advisor as chairman of the board for better company governance - Already implemented with all benefits
  - **Benefits:**
    - **Represent Company at Events** - Chairman speaks on your behalf at conferences/events (saves founder time, reduces opportunity cost)
    - **Crisis Management** - Chairman mitigates negative events (reduces severity of PR scandals, legal issues, regulatory problems by 30-50%)
    - **Enhanced Guidance** - Chairman provides guidance 60% of the time (vs 30% for regular advisors) with 2x impact
    - **Investor Relations** - Reduces board pressure/sentiment issues, improves investor relations
    - **Network Access** - Better partnership opportunities and fundraising connections
  - **Tradeoffs:**
    - **Higher Equity Cost** - Chairman requires 1.5-2x equity (0.5-2% vs 0.25-1% for regular advisor)
    - **Loss of Control** - Chairman has more influence; may disagree with founder decisions (10-20% chance per major decision)
    - **Removal Consequences** - Removing chairman causes negative PR, board sentiment drop, and potential investor concerns
    - **Monthly Costs** - Chairman typically requires $5-15k/month retainer (vs $2-8k for regular advisor)
- ‚úÖ **Remove Advisors** - Ability to remove advisors from the board (with equity buyback options) - Implemented with buyback option and consequences

#### Partner & Channel Programs
