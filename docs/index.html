📈 What is Unicorn?

✨ Features

🎮 Core Gameplay

Invest in 20 startups, track your portfolio in real-time, and experience 60+ random events

🏆 Difficulty Levels

Choose from Easy, Medium, Hard, or Expert mode. Each with unique challenges and rewards

📊 Leaderboards

Compete globally! Track top 10 scores by net worth or ROI with difficulty filters

🎯 35+ Achievements

Unlock achievements across 6 categories from Common to Legendary rarity

📈 Career Progression

Level up from Intern to Legendary Investor with 11 career levels

🏢 Diverse Sectors

Invest across 12+ sectors: FinTech, BioTech, CleanTech, Gaming, and more

🎯 How to Play

1. Download and run the game

2. Choose your difficulty level

3. Review 20 startup options with risk and growth indicators

4. Make strategic investments across different sectors

5. Watch your portfolio grow (or shrink!) over 120 turns

6. Unlock achievements and climb the leaderboards

7. Aim for 1000%+ ROI to earn the "Unicorn Hunter" title!
